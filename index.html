About the app

A focus dashboard built for ambitious people.

lockin is designed for students, creators, editors, developers, and anyone who wants a cleaner relationship with time. It keeps your data offline-first, turns your activity into readable visual patterns, and gives you the structure to go from distracted to deliberate.

The experience is inspired by modern desktop tools: dark glass panels, strong contrast, calm gradients, and a UI that feels sharp instead of noisy. The goal is simple — help you understand your habits, protect your momentum, and keep working on what matters.

Goals Study, editing, blender, coding, custom modes
Analytics Pulse charts, app timelines, productivity labels
Control Timers, alerts, playlists, local backups, theme settings
